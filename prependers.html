The Shakeitoffs

Bio

The Shakeitoffs are a quirky four-piece from Montreal and Toronto, FL. Critics have hailed them as the greatest snth-pop-garage band of the last decade.

They are currently writing a full length album that's a follow-up to their breakout 2011 album, Too many bad apples and their critically acclaimed Ew, Whatever.

Members

  • Jessika Chabot - Vocals, Keyboards
  • Derek - Guitar, Keyboards, Vocals
  • Jessika Gauthier - Drums, Vocals
